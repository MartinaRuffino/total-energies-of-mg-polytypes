How orbitals are ordered by angular momentum L

Index l m Polynomial
1 0 0 1
2 −1 1 y

4 1 1 x

8 2 1 xz
9 2 2 x2−y2
10 3 −3 y(3x2−y2)
11 3 −2 xyz

13 3 0 z(5z2−3)
14 3 1 x(5z2−1)
15 3 2 z(x2-y2)
